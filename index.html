    About Stone Crafters

    At Stone Crafters, we believe your kitchen is the heart of your home. Located in Denver, Colorado, we specialize in bringing bespoke kitchen visions to life through expert design and superior craftsmanship. For over 18 years, our dedicated team has transformed countless homes, focusing on quality materials, meticulous attention to detail, and a seamless client experience from concept to completion. Whether you envision a sleek modern space, a cozy traditional hub, or a custom gourmet chef's kitchen, we partner with you to understand your lifestyle and aesthetic preferences, ensuring a remodel that not only enhances your home's value but also elevates your everyday living. Choose Stone Crafters for a kitchen that is as functional as it is breathtaking.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our services.

    What areas do you serve?

    We provide Kitchen remodeler services throughout Denver, Colorado and surrounding areas. For service areas further out, please contact us to discuss availability.

    What is your booking availability?

    We offer online booking from 7 AM to 6 PM local time daily. Our standard operating days are Sunday to Saturday. Use our online booking system to schedule at your convenience or call (303) 294-0731.

    How should I prepare for my appointment?

    No special preparation is needed! Simply ensure areas are accessible and clear of large obstacles. If you have specific instructions or concerns, mention them when booking. We'll handle the rest, including setup and cleanup.

    Are you licensed and insured?

    Yes! Stone Crafters is fully licensed and fully insured. We take safety seriously and maintain comprehensive liability coverage for all our work. Your home and property are protected with us.

    Do you offer regular maintenance plans?

    Absolutely! We offer flexible maintenance contracts for both residential and commercial clients. Regular upkeep keeps your property looking its best year-round. Bulk maintenance discounts are available. Contact us to discuss a customized plan.

    What eco-friendly practices do you use?

    We use environmentally safe cleaning solutions that are effective on all surfaces. Our methods minimize water waste and avoid harsh chemicals that could harm your landscaping or the environment.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments through our secure online booking system. Payment options are flexible and may include deposit arrangements for larger projects.

    Still have questions?

    If you can't find the answer here, feel free to reach out! Call us at (303) 294-0731 or use our chat assistant on the website. Our team is available to help with any questions about our services.
